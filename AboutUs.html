    ABOUT US

    Established in 1988, Rohan Motors Ltd is a renowned Maruti Suzuki Ltd dealer with a credible track record of performance and customer satisfaction. Over the last 30 years, Rohan Motors Ltd has expanded its presence across Northern India, with 16 Service Locations, 19 Showrooms, and 6 Body shops with state-of-the-art infrastructure. Rohan Motors offers the complete range of services, right from the sale of new cars, exchange and sale of True Value Cars, Genuine Maruti Accessories, Maruti Insurance, and Extended Warranty.

    The Gurgaon outlet of Rohan Motors was inaugurated in the year 2010, and is located on the Golf Course Road, a premium location surrounded by a number of malls and commercial establishments.
